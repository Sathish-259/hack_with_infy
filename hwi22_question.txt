
Number of moves in chessboard
. represent empty cell
R- can move vertically or horizontally
B-can move diagonally
Q-both R and B
Example
3 rows
3 columns
.R.
...
BQ.
 
Output
9

Explanation 
R can move to 0,0
0,2
1,1
But not to 2,1 because it is already occupied by Q
B can move to 1,1
0,2
Q can move to 1,0
1,1
1,2
2,2

Therefore
R-3moves
B-2moves
Q-4moves
